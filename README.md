# Flask Mini Projects Collection

This repository contains **three small web applications built with Flask**:

1. **Age Calculator** – Calculate your age based on birth year and a target year.
2. **Word Counter** – Count words and analyze text entered by the user.
3. **BMI Calculator** – Calculate Body Mass Index (BMI) based on weight and height.

---

## Projects

### 1. Age Calculator
- Enter your birth year and the year you want to calculate age for.
- Click **Calculate** to see your age.
- Backend logic handled by Flask.

### 2. Word Counter
- Enter any text to count words, characters, or analyze text.
- Click **Calculate** to see results.
- Backend logic handled by Flask.

### 3. BMI Calculator
- Enter weight (kg) and height (cm) to calculate BMI.
- Click **Calculate** to see your BMI and category.
- Backend logic handled by Flask.
